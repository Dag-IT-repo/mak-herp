Vision

Be an excellent vehicle of transforming our abundant heritage potential into beneficial resources

Mision

Partner with, and assist, clients and stakeholders in:

  • Turning abundant heritage resources among our communities into beneficial resources that can be enjoyed by all
  • Researching and documenting our rich history and heritage
  • Conserving and preserving our stories by publishing them into books
  • Preserving our rich music – especially indigenous genres – by recording

Background

Mak-Herp prides itself in achieving the following objectives:

  • Make communities aware of the abundant heritage resources among which are potentially beneficial resources that can be tapped into.
  • Research and document rich HISTORY, HERITAGE and CULTURE of communities.
  • Use the researched content of communities’ HISTORY, HERITAGE and CULTURE into literature, dance and music.
  • Conserve and preserve stories and by publishing them into books.
  • use technological and digital devices to conserve and preserve the researched works into dance and music documentary forms on CDs and DVDs.
  • preserve the rich dance and music – especially indigenous genres – by recording them into distributable albums.
  • marketing distribution of the final products of books, CDs and DVDs for public consumption.
